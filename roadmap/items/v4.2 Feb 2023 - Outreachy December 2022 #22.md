# Outreachy December 2022

## Summary

For the first time, Wagtail is joining the Outreachy program. Read our announcement, [Outreachy: welcoming new contributors to open source](https://wagtail.org/blog/outreachy-welcoming-new-contributors-to-open-source/).

## Intended Outcome

We onboard new contributors into open source.

## How will it work?

There will be multiple internships focused on delivering improvements in Wagtail for the February 2022 release, supported by mentors from our community.

- See [Outreachy: welcoming new contributors to open source](https://wagtail.org/blog/outreachy-welcoming-new-contributors-to-open-source/).
